About Project

Built for students,
by student

Study Stream was born from the frustration of cluttered productivity apps. We stripped away everything that doesn't serve your focus, leaving you with a clean, powerful tool that just works.

Built with Electron, it runs natively on your desktop with minimal resource usage. No browser tabs, no notifications, no excuses.
