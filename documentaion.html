What is Study Stream Black?

Study Stream Black is a free, offline-first desktop application built for serious learners. It transforms your downloaded courses into a Netflix-style library, gives you a custom video player built for learning (not entertainment), lets your AI tutor read your course subtitles and answer your questions in real time, and connects you with a leaderboard of scholars grinding alongside you.

Core Philosophy

Your focus environment should be as powerful as your courses. No browser tabs, no notifications, no excuses.

Pillar What it means
Offline-First Your courses live on your disk. No internet required to study.
AI-Powered Your AI reads your course subtitles and answers contextual questions.
Time-Synced Notes Notes are anchored to the exact second in the video.
Gamified XP, levels, streaks, and a live leaderboard keep you accountable.

Windows Security Warning

Don't Panic!

Windows may show a "Windows protected your PC" warning. This is normal because the app is not code-signed (certificates cost hundreds of dollars). The app is 100% open-source.

How to bypass:

  1. Click "More info" below the warning text.
  2. Click the "Run anyway" button that appears.
  3. The installer will proceed normally.

Setting Up AI (Gemini)

The AI assistant is the crown jewel of Study Stream. To use it, you need to connect your own Google Gemini API key.

Getting Your API Key

Google Gemini offers a generous free tier ‚Äî no credit card needed.

  1. Go to Google AI Studio: aistudio.google.com/app/apikey
  2. Sign in with Google.
  3. Click "Create API Key".
  4. Copy the key (starts with AIzaSy...).

Choosing the Model

Go to Settings ‚Üí AI Settings in the app, paste your key, and select a model:

Tier Model Recommendation
Free gemma-3-27b-it Best free choice. Excellent for Q&A.
Paid gemini-2.0-flash Best balance of speed and quality.

07

The Video Player

The video player is purpose-built for learning. Every feature exists to help you study better.

Smart Notes & Bookmarks

Notes in Study Stream aren't just text ‚Äî they're timestamped anchors.

  • Press Ctrl + N to take a note at the current time.
  • Press B to bookmark a moment.
  • Clicking a note later jumps the video to that exact second.
08

AI Study Assistant

The AI has multiple modes. The most powerful is Course Chat Mode.

How it works

When you open the chat while watching a video, the AI automatically reads the subtitle file. It knows the full transcript of the lesson, allowing you to ask contextual questions like "Explain that last concept differently" or "Quiz me on this section."

10

XP, Levels & Clearance

Every meaningful action earns XP (Experience Points). Your XP fills a progress bar toward the next level.

  • Watching video: ~5 XP/min
  • Completing a course: +500 XP bonus
  • Daily streak: +25 XP

As you level up, your Clearance Level increases, unlocking premium themes in the store.

11

Hall of Fame

A real-time global leaderboard where scholars compete based on total study hours and XP.

Privacy Note

To appear on the leaderboard, you must set a Scholar Handle in Settings. You can toggle your visibility at any time.
